Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How much time does packing take for a 2BHK?
For a standard 2BHK, a team of 4 professionals typically takes 4–6 hours to dismantle, pack, and inventory all items before loading.
Do you provide transit insurance?
Yes! We provide comprehensive transit insurance on all shipments. In the rare case of damage, you are fully covered based on the declared value of goods.
Are there any hidden charges?
Absolutely not. We pride ourselves on 100% transparent pricing. The quote after our pre-move survey is final — inclusive of all taxes, tolls, and packing materials.
Do you unpack and assemble furniture at the destination?
Yes, our door-to-door service includes unpacking heavy furniture and placing items as per your instruction, along with disposal of packing debris.
How can I track my shipment?
We provide real-time GPS tracking for all inter-city moves. You'll receive a tracking link once your shipment is dispatched. Our support team is also available 24/7 for updates.
